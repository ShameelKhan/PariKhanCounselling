About me

General Information

I am a person-centred counsellor accredited by the BACP and IAPT qualified to high intensity level in working with depression. I have several years' experience in the NHS primary care sector in both short and long-term counselling, seeing adult clients presenting with a wide range of issues ranging from bereavement, stress and anxiety to relationships, cultural and identity issues. I am also able to work in an integrative way by combining elements of CBT where appropriate. My additional languages are Urdu and Hindi.

Approach to counselling

I believe that my role as a counsellor is to facilitate the client to explore their problems in the context of their daily lives and relationships and to deepen their understanding of themselves. By enhancing their self-awareness, making links with the past and looking at how this translates into their presenting issues, the client is then able to find their own solutions in a confidential and non-judgemental environment.
